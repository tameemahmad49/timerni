About Us

Times research & innovation is a research organisation based in the United Kingdom and Bangladesh. We have started various research in behavioural computer sciences; technology, health informatics education and community research. Our supreme emphasis is to execute research, training and development activities initially in the UK and Bangladesh to help disadvantaged people. Our Chair and CEO Shah Siddiqui is currently working on ‘ANGTHAS’ an Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning project as his PhD research at the University of Portsmouth, UK.

Currently, we are developing World Health AID , a commercial telemedicine platform for ANGTHAS to start operations for the mass population. It will offer free and paid services through web and mobile applications. The motivation of the work is to establish a worldwide healthcare system for underprivileged and disabled people at hard to reach areas affected by poverty, war and natural calamities. All our other innovative project’s details can be found on the project area of this website.

Our unique focus on upcoming projects is to take care of the environment, vulnerable and deprived people in the society who are for instance discriminated against for their sexual orientations and beliefs. By our research, we will point out the gaps and will draw a strategic plan to resolve those. We know it is a collective responsibility and can not do it alone. Therefore, we will create a support bubble with our partners and collaborators to strike together.

We will combine modern technologies, Expert Systems and Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Data Science, Computer Vision and Imaging, Natural Language Process (NLP), and Internet of things (IoT) in the heart of our solutions. Together we can bridge the gap between culture, race, communities and societies. We would highly appreciate your support to educate and empower them to make a difference in society and the economy.
